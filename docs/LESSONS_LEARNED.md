# Lessons Learned

## Recent success is not enough

The 2025 results were useful, but they did not survive 5-year and 10-year checks. A research method must widen the time horizon before any promotion decision.

## Risk overlays need proof

Adding a defensive layer or a cost filter can reduce some exposure while also removing good trades. The result must be tested as a complete system, not as an intuitive patch.

## Negative results are research assets

Rejected hypotheses make future work faster because they reduce repeated testing of ideas that already failed public gates.

## Public archives need boundaries

Raw data, broker artifacts and account materials do not belong in the public repository. The public value should come from sanitized evidence, reproducible method and clear limitations.
