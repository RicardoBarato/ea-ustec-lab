## Robustness expansion

The research then expanded from a recent positive window into 2021-2025 and 2016-2025 checks. Both main candidates failed those longer windows.

## Rejection phase

Regime stand-aside, cost/spread gates, reward-target changes and risk expansion were evaluated as research hypotheses. None produced enough evidence for promotion.

## Public archive phase
