# Continuation Guide

## Recommended direction

Future work should begin with a new entry thesis or regime-first architecture rather than tuning the rejected candidates.

## Suggested protocol

1. Define the hypothesis in one paragraph.
2. Define the data contract and validation windows before testing.
3. Include transaction-cost assumptions early.
4. Compare 2025, 2021-2025 and 2016-2025 from the beginning.
5. Use walk-forward and multi-broker reproduction before promotion.
6. Publish positive and negative observations together.
7. Keep operational approval separate from research usefulness.

## Acceptance mindset

A candidate can be useful research and still fail promotion. The goal is better evidence, not a better-looking single window.
