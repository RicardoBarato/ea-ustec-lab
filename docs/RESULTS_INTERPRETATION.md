# Results Interpretation

## How to read the public metrics

Net profit, profit factor and drawdown should be interpreted together. A positive net result does not imply robustness when PF is thin or drawdown expands across longer windows.

## Long-horizon interpretation

The 2021-2025 and 2016-2025 windows were negative for both main candidates. That means the public decision is not robust, even though the 2025 window remains valuable evidence.

## What not to infer

Do not infer live performance, broker-independent transferability or operational approval from these figures. They are historical backtest observations.
